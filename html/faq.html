General Questions

What do I get with my subscription?

You'll receive two surprise, curated arrangements from us every month. We also deliver it right to your door.

Can I pick my own flowers?

No, it's a surprise! We deliver you exquisite arrangements curated by our designers. Check out our Past Refreshes page to get an idea of what to expect.

Delivery

Who delivers my flowers?

We do! Our service is unique in that we do all of our own fulfillment. This allows us to keep your flowers as fresh as possible.

What if I live in an apartment?

No problem! We do our best to deliver to your door. But since we do our own delivery, there are cases where not having access to the property can be a challenge.

Our checkout page offers the ability to provide specific delivery instructions to our staff. In most cases, this is sufficient. But if you suspect that even with this it might be an issue, we ask that you consider having your flowers delivered to another location - like your workplace, for instance.

Can I change my shipping address?

Yes, you can change your shipping address at anytime. It will be applied to your next order, provided it is changed 1 day prior to scheduled delivery. Contact Customer Support.

How often and when are my flowers delivered?

We have two ordering options:

  • Twice a Week

    • Flowers are delivered on the 1st and 3rd Tuesday of every month.

  • Every Week

    • Flowers are delivered every week on Mondays.

Gift Orders

How do I buy a gift for someone?

We have special options for gifting! Simply click the "Give a Gift" link on the navigation bar or go here.

How are gift orders different from subscriptions?

First off, every gift order gets a vase! Second, they are non-recurring, meaning you are not rebilled. Third, you can order gifts off-cycle, meaning the first arrangement will be delivered on the next delivery date (1st or 3rd Tuesday of the month). And last, the first delivery includes a personalized note printed on linen paper.

Do I have to wait until next month to send a gift?

We answered this above. But we feel it's important enough to reiterate!

NO, you do not have to wait. You can order gifts off-cycle, meaning the first arrangement will be delivered on the next delivery date (1st or 3rd Tuesday of the month). Take a look at our delivery dates to see when the next delivery is.

Is there a deadline to order gifts?

While there is no "cycle" for gift deliveries, you still must order 48 hours before the listed delivery date.

Orders/Billing

When am I billed?

All subscriptions are billed on the 19th of the month.

If it's passed the 19th, can I still get next month's refreshes?

Yes, we generally allow for sign ups 2 days prior to that month's first refresh.

How do I cancel?

We hope it doesn't come to this. But if you need to cancel for any reason, you may contact Customer Support and we'll get you squared away.

Can I skip deliveries?

Yes, you're able to skip your bundle deliveries on a month-by-month basis. Please notify Customer Support. Please note that we will automatically resume billing the following month unless we're otherwise notified.

Do you offer pro-rated subscription?

There are no pro-rated subscriptions at this time.
