PRIVACY STATEMENT

Last updated on September 26, 2015

This Privacy Statement explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of certain information, including your personal information, by Novoot LLC (the "Company," "we," "ours," "Novoot," or "us").

1. Collection of Information

Novoot LLC, collects both "personal information" and "non-personal information" about website users ("users," "you," or "your"). For purposes of this Policy, "non-personal information" is information that we cannot directly associate with a specific person without the aid of additional information. By contrast, "personal information," is information such as a name or email address that we can directly associate with a specific person or entity without additional information. When we combine non-personal information with personal information, we treat all of the combined information as personal information.

2. Use of Information

You can visit the Company website (the "Website") without submitting any personal information. However, in order to become a user of our monthly subscription service for bi-monthly delivery of flower bundles (the "Product") to you ("Subscription"), you will be required to provide personal information.

2.1 Information you provide us with

We collect information you provide to us, which includes but is not limited to:

  • Full name;
  • Email address;
  • Complete physical address;
  • Payment method;
  • Phone number; and
  • Information collected when you choose to provide reviews or ratings.

2.2 Information we collect automatically

We collect information regarding you and your use of our services. This information includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your activity on the Company service;
  • Details regarding your interactions with customer service, such as the date, time and reason for contacting us, the email address you use to contact us and the content of any emails addressed to us;
  • Device IDs or unique identifiers, device and software characteristics (such as type and configuration), connection information, statistics on page views, referral URLs, ad data, IP address and standard web log information; and
  • Information collected via the use of cookies, web beacons and other technologies.

2.3 Information from other sources

We might supplement the information described above with information we obtain from other sources, including from both online and offline data providers. Such supplemental information could include demographic data, interest based data, and Internet browsing behavior.

2.4 How we use the Information

We use the information we collect to provide, analyze, administer, enhance and personalize our services and marketing efforts, to process your registration, your orders and your payments, and to communicate with you on these and other topics. For example, we use the information we collect for the following non-exhaustive list:

  • Determining your general geographic location and determining your Internet service provider and helping us quickly and efficiently respond to inquiries and requests;
  • Preventing, detecting and investigating potentially prohibited or illegal activities, including fraud, and enforcing our terms (such as determining free trial eligibility);
  • Improving our service (including our user interface experiences); and
  • Communicating with you concerning our service.

8. Cookies and Internet Advertising

We may also use cookies in conjunction with the Website. Cookies are small files that are stored on your computer or other device by your web browser. A cookie allows the Website to recognize whether you have visited before and may store user preferences and other information. For example, cookies can be used to collect information about your use of the Website during your current session and over time (including the pages you view and the files you download), the operating system and browser type of your computer or other device, your Internet service provider, your domain name and IP address, your general geographic location, the website that you visited before navigating to the Platforms, and the link you used to leave the Platforms. If you are concerned about having cookies on your computer or device, you can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being set, allowing you to decide whether to accept it. You can also delete cookies from your computer or device. However, if you choose to block or delete cookies, certain features of the Website may not operate correctly.
